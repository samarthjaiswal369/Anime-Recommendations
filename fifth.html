Genre:- Romance


Level-1:- Clannad: After Story




Description:-

Clannad: After Story is a heartfelt continuation of Tomoya Okazaki’s journey — a young man who once drifted through life aimlessly, carrying the emotional scars of a troubled family and a lonely past. After meeting Nagisa Furukawa, a kind and gentle girl with fragile health but an unshakable spirit, Tomoya’s world slowly begins to change. Together, they navigate the final days of high school, supporting each other’s dreams and overcoming the weight of their insecurities. As they graduate and step into adulthood, their bond deepens, and they face the harsh realities of life head-on — love, work, marriage, and the struggles that come with growing up. What begins as a simple school romance soon transforms into a profound exploration of family, loss, resilience, and unconditional love. Through laughter and heartbreak, Tomoya learns the true meaning of connection and discovers that even in the darkest moments, life can still hold beauty and hope. The story’s emotional depth and breathtaking storytelling remind viewers that love is not just about happiness — it’s about enduring together, no matter how painful the journey becomes. With its tearful moments, powerful life lessons, and deeply human characters, Clannad: After Story stands as one of the most emotionally moving romance anime ever created — a timeless reminder that even after tragedy, love can rebuild what was once lost.



Level-2:- Kaguya-sama: Love Is War




Description:-

At the prestigious Shuchiin Academy, where only the most brilliant and elite students gather, two geniuses stand at the very top — Kaguya Shinomiya, the elegant and calculating vice president, and Miyuki Shirogane, the hardworking and sharp-minded student council president. Both admired by everyone around them, they seem like the perfect couple — and in truth, they are madly in love with each other. But there’s one problem: their pride. Neither Kaguya nor Miyuki is willing to confess their feelings first, believing that doing so would mean “losing” in the game of love. What follows is a hilarious and surprisingly heartfelt psychological battle, where every interaction — a simple compliment, a glance, or even a text — becomes a strategic war move. With the help (and chaos) of their quirky friends like Chika Fujiwara, the bubbly secretary, and Yu Ishigami, the cynical treasurer, their student council turns into a comedic battleground of schemes, misunderstandings, and emotional tension. As the story unfolds, Kaguya-sama: Love is War evolves from a witty romantic comedy into something much deeper — a tale about vulnerability, growing up, and learning to open one’s heart. Behind the playful mind games lie two people who are genuinely afraid of rejection, yet drawn to each other by an unshakable bond. Through laughter, rivalry, and tender moments, they slowly begin to break down the walls around their hearts, proving that love isn’t about victory or defeat — it’s about honesty and courage. Blending sharp humor, heartfelt emotion, and beautiful character growth, Kaguya-sama: Love is War stands as one of the smartest and most charming romance anime ever made — a battle where the first to confess truly wins.



Level-3 Your Lie in April




Description:-

Your Lie in April is a hauntingly beautiful story about love, music, and the courage to live again after loss. It follows Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy once hailed as a child genius. Trained harshly by his strict mother, Kousei played with mechanical perfection — until her death shattered his spirit. Traumatized, he lost the ability to hear the sound of his own piano, even though his hearing was perfectly fine. His world, once filled with music, turned silent and colorless. Everything changes the day he meets Kaori Miyazono, a free-spirited violinist whose wild and passionate performances break every rule of classical music. Kaori is everything Kousei is not — spontaneous, fearless, and full of life. She drags him back into the world of music, challenging him to play not with precision, but with heart. Through her, Kousei begins to rediscover the joy, pain, and beauty that music — and life — can bring. But as their bond deepens, Kousei starts to realize that Kaori is hiding a secret — one that will forever change both of their lives. Your Lie in April transforms from a simple love story into a heartbreaking symphony about dreams, loss, and the people who inspire us to move forward even when life feels unbearable. Every note in this story echoes with emotion — laughter, tears, regret, and hope — reminding us that love isn’t always forever, but its melody stays in our hearts long after the song ends. With breathtaking animation, emotional depth, and a timeless message, Your Lie in April is not just an anime — it’s a masterpiece that makes you feel every note of life’s most beautiful symphony.
